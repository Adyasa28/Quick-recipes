Schezwan freid rice

15 mins • Comfort Food • Healthy(if quantity of vegetables are good)

Home Recipes Why Quick Recipes About 📝 Suggest

🌱Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (cooled)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, capsicum, peas)
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 tbsp Schezwan sauce
  • 1 tsp soy sauce (optional)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

🍳 Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok on high flame. Add chopped garlic and sauté briefly.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry on high for 2–3 minutes (keep them slightly crunchy).
  3. Add the cooked rice, Schezwan sauce, soy sauce (if using), salt and pepper.
  4. Toss well on high heat for 2–3 minutes until everything is combined. Serve hot.
