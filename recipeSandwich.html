Masala Sandwich

15 mins • Easy Snack • Tasty & Filling

Home Recipes Why Quick Recipes About 📝 Suggest

🌱Ingredients

  • 4 bread slices
  • 2 tsp butter
  • 2 tsp green chutney (optional)
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • Salt & black pepper to taste

🍳 Steps

  1. Spread butter and chutney (if using) on each bread slice.
  2. Place cucumber, tomato and onion slices on a slice. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  3. Top with another slice of bread. Toast or grill if desired.
  4. Cut in halves and serve immediately.
