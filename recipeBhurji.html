Paneer Bhurji

10 mins • Under ₹70 • Protein-rich

Home Recipes About 📝 Suggest

🌱Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (crumbled)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 Tomato (chopped)
  • 1 Green chilli
  • 1 tsp Haldi, Red Chilli, Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste

🍳 Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add onion & chilli.
  2. Add tomatoes & spices, cook till soft.
  3. Mix in crumbled paneer, stir for 2–3 mins.
  4. Serve hot with roti or bread.
