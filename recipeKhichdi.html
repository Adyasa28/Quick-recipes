Dal Khichdi

25 mins • Comfort Food • Healthy

Home Recipes Why Quick Recipes About 📝 Suggest

🌱Ingredients

  • Rice – ½ cup
  • Moong dal (yellow split) – ½ cup
  • Turmeric – ¼ tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Ghee – 1 tbsp
  • Cumin seeds – ½ tsp
  • Hing (asafoetida) – a pinch
  • Ginger (optional) – ½ tsp grated

🍳 Steps

  1. Wash rice & dal, pressure cook with 3 cups water, salt & turmeric (2–3 whistles).
  2. Heat ghee, add cumin, hing, ginger – sauté.
  3. Pour this tadka over cooked dal-rice mix.
  4. Mix & serve with papad or pickle.
